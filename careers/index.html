Build careers, not jobs.

What we hire for

Four kinds of people we are looking for.

These are the roles we hire for most often. If you do not see a fit but think you belong here, send a note anyway.

Senior support agent

You have run a support inbox before, ideally for a software or service business. You write clean replies, you can hold a Zoom call without freezing, and you actually like solving customer problems. We place you with one client and give you a real seat on their team.

Account manager

You lead a group of client accounts, sit in on quarterly reviews, coach agents, and keep our placements going strong. Great people skills, calm under pressure, and a real interest in how the businesses we serve actually work. This role is the heart of what we do.

AI workflows specialist

You build and tune the AI tools that work alongside our agents. Writing AI prompts, light coding, connecting tools together, and turning messy human work into something repeatable. Comfortable with tools like Zapier, OpenAI APIs, and a new CRM you have never used before.

Operations and team lead

You help us run things behind the scenes. Hiring pipelines, onboarding, payroll, internal tools, and the career path our agents follow. The right person here treats operations as a craft, not a chore.

What it is like to work here

The short version: we run xFusion like the place we always wanted to work.

We pay a living wage, on purpose.

Our agents are paid well above the local market rate for support work. That sounds simple, but in this industry it is rare. It is the single biggest reason our retention numbers do not look like a typical staffing firm.

Culture is a line item, not an afterthought.

We invest in team-building, swag, anniversaries, structured holiday gifting, birthday recognition, and the kind of small things that make a remote job feel like it actually counts. Our account managers spend real time on this.

Most people stay for years, not months.

Most of our team has been here for years, not months. That is the bar we hire to. We are not running a turn-and-burn shop. We are building careers worth keeping, for people who want to grow into senior support, operations, and leadership roles.

We are not a typical call center.

Our agents work with one client at a time. They join that client's daily team meetings, learn the product, and act like part of the team. A typical call center rotates agents across customers and treats them as replaceable parts. We do the opposite.

Where we work

Three regions, one team.

Most of our team is in the Philippines, with a growing group in Kenya and a small operations crew in the United States. We hire remote first, and we ship physical swag to every region a few times a year.

Annual in-person team-building happens regionally. Last year, that meant a beach retreat outside Manila and a weekend in Nairobi. The kind of thing people actually post about.

Get in touch

We are always interested in talking to people who care about doing real support work.

Send us a note, even if we are not advertising your role. We read every email.
